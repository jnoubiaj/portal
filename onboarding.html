CapitalQuest – Funding Agreement

Client Services Agreement · CapitalQuest Funding LLC

The undersigned                      (name of Client), hereafter known as "Client", appoints and authorizes CapitalQuest Funding LLC, known as the "Consultant" or "Company", to find lender(s) and to submit financial data and information supplied by the Client, for the purpose of obtaining 0% interest personal or business credit cards, lines of credit, and/or term loans. CapitalQuest Funding is keenly aware of those lenders who are seeking customers with a specific credit profile. The approach for financing taken will be based upon the information supplied by the Client. Once the client is "cleared for funding" Client agrees to complete the soon to be provided "Questionnaire" truthfully to the best of their knowledge.

Program Outline

  • 1 on 1 onboarding call with the Team to discuss funding rounds.
  • We will provide Client 0% interest business funding, loans, business lines of credit, and/or business term loan funding.
  • We will provide Client access to our step by step process on how to get funding on their own after they are approved from their funding rounds.
  • Direct access to the Team for any updates or questions.
  • Specifically, we will not apply for any short-term loans, credit facilities, or credit cards that accrue interest without obtaining explicit permission from the client involved. This policy underscores our commitment to upholding our clients' best interests and maintaining a high standard of fiduciary responsibility. Applications are completed by Consultant both online and over the phone. Some applications may require the client to go in person to the bank as directed by the Consultant in pursuit to help the Client achieve funding for their business.
  • Upon completion of the funding process, all client data will be deleted from our records. This includes personal identification information, financial records, and any other sensitive data collected during the funding process. This measure is taken to ensure the privacy and security of our clients' information.

Payment

The Client agrees to pay CapitalQuest Funding LLC for the 0% interest funding service and consulting.

The Client understands there may be additional costs to help optimize their personal credit profile. This will be determined during the optimization phase and does not apply to everyone. These costs include but are not limited to: paying credit card usage down, paying derogatory collections on the report as directed, adding authorized user tradelines, maintaining required credit monitoring, paying for any business credit reports deemed necessary, and anything else deemed necessary in the pursuit of preparing the client for funding during the optimization phase. In the event the client needs authorized user tradelines to bolster their credit report, this will be invoiced separately.

Consultant is not an employee of any financial institution and is not authorized to make any financial commitments on behalf of any Lender. The APR (annual percentage rate of interest charged by the lender) of all financing is determined individually by scoring; underwriting methods utilized by each lender. There are no guarantees made by the Consultant as to the terms of your approvals.

Client understands CapitalQuest Funding LLC shall not be held responsible or liable for any losses, damages, or consequences that may arise due to the client's default on any credit facilities, including but not limited to business credit cards, loans, or lines of credit, obtained with the assistance of CapitalQuest Funding LLC. No Chargeback Clause

The Client agrees not to initiate any chargeback on the investment paid to CapitalQuest Funding LLC. In the event of a chargeback, the Client will be subject to legal action, and the Company will pursue all necessary legal remedies to recover the chargeback amount.

Additional Terms

The Client also agrees to create a MyScoreIQ or Experian 3-bureau account and provide CapitalQuest Funding LLC with logins and passwords for the purpose of the Consultant making best recommendations to the Client and monitoring updates that will help the Client optimize their personal credit profile. Failure to have an active MyScoreIQ account or provide valid login will result in delayed funding results. Client also agrees to not apply for any uninstructed revolving credit accounts until the funding service is complete, as this would negatively impact Client's funding results. If Client needs to apply for credit products such as auto loans or a mortgage during this process, it's important that the client notify the Consultant, as the extra inquiries could have a negative impact on funding results.
