Privacy Policy

At CapitalQuest Funding, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Financial Data: Credit history, banking relationships, and funding details.
Login Credentials: Username and password for accessing our portal.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to assess creditworthiness and develop funding strategies, submit and track funding applications with banks, provide updates on your funding status via our portal, and improve our services.
